## What I'm building

**[AGRIVISION AI](https://github.com/agrivisionai-org)** is an AI-first technology company building intelligent products for global agriculture.

Our flagship platform, **YieldAI Global**, is an AI-powered crop intelligence system for the extension workers and farming households who feed the world. Designed for 40+ countries and 70+ languages -- starting with India, Africa, Latin America, and Southeast Asia.

Extension workers are the backbone of global food security -- each one advises 1,200+ farming households. Yet most work without real-time data, AI tools, or automated reporting. YieldAI changes that.

## What I work on

- Multilingual voice-first AI for low-resource languages (Telugu, Bhojpuri, Swahili, Bengali, Kannada, and 65+ more)
- Retrieval-grounded vision for agronomic pest and disease identification
- Multi-agent systems with hard refusal logic -- knowing when *not* to answer is the hard part
- LLM evaluation harnesses with native-speaker agronomist review
